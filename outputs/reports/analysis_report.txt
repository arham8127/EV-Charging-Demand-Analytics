EV Charging Station Demand Analytics Report

Project Objective
Analyze electric vehicle adoption trends and charging infrastructure data.

Datasets Used
1. EV Population Dataset
2. Charging Stations Dataset

Analysis Performed
- EV Growth Trend Analysis
- Top EV Brands Analysis
- EV Type Distribution
- Electric Range Distribution
- Charging Station Distribution
- Charging Station Map Visualization

Key Insights
- EV adoption is increasing every year.
- Tesla is one of the leading EV brands.
- Battery Electric Vehicles dominate the EV market.
- Fast chargers are the most commonly used charging stations.

Tools Used
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Folium

Conclusion
EV adoption is rapidly growing and charging infrastructure will need to expand to meet future demand.
